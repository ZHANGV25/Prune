1. The app

Prune helps you review the photos in your Apple Photos library with a swipe-to-keep / swipe-to-delete interface, and then bulk-removes the photos you chose to delete through Apple's standard Photos framework. Deleted photos go to your device's Recently Deleted album, where Apple retains them for 30 days before permanent removal.
